Developers:
Gassa (Ivan Kazmenko)
naagi (Natalya Ginzburg)

Dependencies:
Allegro 5.1+
dmd 2.070+
DAllegro5 by SiegeLord

Game:
You fight winter by crafting a spring poem by a known author.

Rules:
Crafting: assemble a verse.
Rhyme: the verse itself.
Snow: fight against winter by reading the verse out loud!
Pathfinding: find where the pieces belong in the puzzle.
Unicode: Russian and English poems included.
